Oncology estimand working group: newsletter 17th July 2023

Hi all,

We hope this reaches you well! Before we all leave for summer vacation we would like to share again some recent developments, events, and achievements.

1 Updates

  • Since the last newsletter the following publications have been accepted or already appeared:
    • Rufibach et al. (2023), including code.
    • Liu et al. (2023) .
    • Englert et al. (2023) .
  • We have updated the events page with our past and upcoming appearances, including posting of presented slidedecks. Please let us know of further presentations, workshops, webinars, etc that are not on the list yet!
  • Conferences: the working group contributed
    • a session to the PSI 2023 conference in London. Hong Tian, Konstantina Skaltsa, Stefan Englert, Lynda Grinsted, Elizabeth Pilling, and Yufei Wang presented outcomes from various taskforces to a completely full room.
    • even organized two sessions at the LIDS 2023 conference in Raleigh.
    • co-organized and contributed to a BBS seminar on Quantification of risk: ask the right questions or time to apply the estimand framework to safety!.
    • Jonathan gave an invited talk at the Duke industry statistics symposium 2023.
